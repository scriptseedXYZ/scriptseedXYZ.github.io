Healthcare Proactive Case Management

  • Problem: Preventable readmissions and deteriorations are not flagged early enough, leading to higher cost of care and poorer outcomes.
  • Solution: Prototype ML model that scores patients for risk and triggers proactive case management workflows (outreach, review queues) via a simple dashboard.
  • Value (est.): Illustrates how a 10–20% earlier intervention rate could translate into significant cost savings and better patient outcomes in a mid‑size health system.

AI Assisted Document Search

  • Problem: Analysts and operators lose time searching through dense documents, policies, and tickets to answer routine questions.
  • Solution: GenAI‑powered search prototype combining semantic retrieval and Q&A so users can ask natural‑language questions and get answers with cited sources.
  • Value (est.): Demonstrates how a 30–40% reduction in search time for knowledge workers could free up hundreds of hours per year in a single team.

Petrochem_Utility_Efficiency_Optimization

  • Problem: Petrochemical plants were operating utilities (steam, power, cooling, etc.) with limited visibility into real-time efficiency, leading to higher energy costs and difficulty spotting optimization opportunities.
  • Solution: Designed and implemented a Petrochem Utility Efficiency Optimization app that ingests operations data, benchmarks utility performance, and surfaces actionable recommendations to operators through intuitive dashboards and alerts.
  • Value (est.): Enabled data-driven utility optimization, identifying efficiency improvements that can reduce energy consumption and operating costs while improving reliability and decision-making for plant leadership.
